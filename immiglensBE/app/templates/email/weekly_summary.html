Disclaimer & Important Information

About This Digest

This weekly summary is sent every Monday morning and covers activity from the prior calendar week (Monday 00:00 UTC to Sunday 23:59 UTC). Data shown reflects the state of your ImmigLens account at the time of report generation. Activity that occurred after the generation time will appear in next week’s digest. Weekly summaries are sent regardless of whether any activity occurred . a digest showing zero captures may indicate a misconfiguration worth investigating.

Data Accuracy

The statistics in this digest are drawn from ImmigLens’s internal capture and evidence logs. While ImmigLens strives for accuracy in all reported figures, minor discrepancies may occur due to time zone differences, in-progress capture runs at the time of report generation, or edge cases in error classification. For authoritative evidence records, always refer to the Evidence Timeline in your ImmigLens dashboard rather than the digest figures.

Attention Items Disclaimer

The ‘Items that need your attention’ section is generated automatically based on rule-based checks (failed captures, expired URLs, long periods of inactivity). These flags are informational and do not constitute a legal assessment of whether your LMIA evidence is complete or compliant. The absence of flagged items in this digest does not guarantee that your evidence is sufficient for ESDC review. ImmigLens recommends reviewing all evidence manually with your RCIC or immigration lawyer before submitting an LMIA application.

Notification Preferences

You are receiving this weekly summary because the Weekly Digest option is enabled in your account’s Notification Settings. You can switch to monthly digests, disable the digest entirely, or configure which types of events are included, from Account Settings → Notifications. Disabling the weekly digest does not affect transactional notifications such as capture failure alerts, payment notifications, or security emails.

CASL Compliance

This weekly activity digest is a commercial electronic message as defined under Canada’s Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL). You may unsubscribe from digest emails at any time using the link below. Unsubscribing from the digest does not unsubscribe you from transactional or security emails, which are sent based on account activity and cannot be disabled while your account is active.
