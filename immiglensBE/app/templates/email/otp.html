Code Security & Expiry

Verification codes are single-use and expire after {{ expire_minutes }} minutes from the time of generation. Once used, a code cannot be reused. ImmigLens generates these codes using a cryptographically secure random number generator. Codes are hashed at rest and are never stored in plain text. ImmigLens staff have no ability to view or retrieve your one-time code. If your code expires, you must request a new one from the sign-in screen.

Phishing & Fraud Warning

ImmigLens will never ask you to share your verification code, password, or billing information via email, phone, or chat. If you receive a message claiming to be from ImmigLens that asks for your code, do not respond . it is a phishing attempt. Forward suspicious emails to support@immiglens.ca. ImmigLens will never initiate unsolicited contact asking you to verify your identity through a link in an email without a corresponding action on your part.

Account Takeover Protection

If you did not initiate this verification request and receive multiple codes in a short period, your email address may be targeted by automated account-creation attempts. ImmigLens rate-limits verification requests to reduce abuse. Contact support@immiglens.ca if you believe your email address is being used without your consent.
