{# Understanding active vs archived #}

Understanding active vs. archived positions

•  Active — currently in recruitment; capture schedules are running; counts toward your plan limit
•  Paused — temporarily inactive; capture schedules are paused; still counts toward your plan limit
•  Archived — recruitment period has ended; no captures run; does NOT count toward your plan limit
•  Completed — reports have been generated and the position is closed; does NOT count toward your plan limit

Disclaimer & Important Information

Plan Limit Enforcement

ImmigLens plan limits apply to the number of Job Positions with a status of Active at any given time. The limit is enforced at the time of position creation — if you are at the limit, the creation of a new position will be blocked with an on-screen error message and this email notification. Positions that are archived, completed, or deleted do not count toward the active limit, and archiving a position immediately frees up a slot. ImmigLens does not retroactively remove or archive positions on your behalf to stay within plan limits.

Upgrading Mid-Cycle

If you upgrade your plan during an active billing period, the new plan limits take effect immediately. The additional charge for the upgrade is prorated based on the number of days remaining in the current billing cycle. The prorated amount is charged to your payment method on file and reflected on your next invoice.

Downgrading & Immediate Position Deactivation

If you downgrade to a plan with fewer active position slots, the change takes effect immediately upon confirmation — not at the end of the billing cycle. At the moment of downgrade, all of your currently active positions are automatically set to Inactive, regardless of how many positions you have or whether they are within the new plan’s limit. This is a system-wide reset to ensure no account exceeds the new plan limit. Once the downgrade is processed, you must manually go into your Positions dashboard and reactivate the positions you wish to keep active, up to the limit of your new plan. Positions that remain inactive do not count toward your plan limit, and their capture schedules remain paused until you reactivate them. No evidence, screenshots, or reports are deleted when a position is deactivated — the full evidence history is preserved. ImmigLens strongly recommends deciding in advance which positions you intend to reactivate before confirming a downgrade, particularly if you are in an active LMIA recruitment period, as capture schedules will stop on all positions the moment the downgrade is confirmed.

LMIA Workflow Continuity

Reaching a plan limit during an active LMIA application period could disrupt your ability to track new positions in ImmigLens. If you anticipate needing additional positions for upcoming LMIA work, we recommend upgrading proactively rather than waiting until the limit is reached. ImmigLens usage-threshold warning emails are sent when you reach 80% of your plan limit to help you plan ahead.
