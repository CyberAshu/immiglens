Disclaimer & Important Information

About Capture Failures

Capture failures can occur for a variety of reasons, including changes to the target website, temporary network issues, platform-side bot detection, and DNS or SSL errors. ImmigLens makes best-effort attempts to capture screenshots on schedule and performs automatic retries on failure. ImmigLens does not guarantee capture success for every scheduled run, as external factors outside the control of the platform may prevent access to source URLs.

Evidence Gap

A gap in captured evidence does not automatically disqualify an LMIA application. ESDC reviewers assess the overall recruitment effort and may accept supplementary evidence such as job board invoices, confirmation emails, and screenshots taken by other means. Users are responsible for identifying and addressing any gaps in their evidence record before submitting an LMIA application. ImmigLens does not notify ESDC or any regulatory authority of capture failures. ImmigLens strongly recommends consulting an RCIC or immigration lawyer if you have concerns about the completeness of your recruitment evidence.

Manual Evidence Upload

Where automated capture has failed, users may upload manual screenshots from the Evidence Timeline. Manual uploads are logged with the date and time of upload, and include a field to record the original date of the screenshot. Users are responsible for the accuracy and authenticity of manually uploaded evidence. ImmigLens applies the same SHA-256 hash integrity check to manually uploaded files as it does to automated captures.

Retry Policy

ImmigLens retries failed captures automatically up to {{ max_retries }} times. If all retries fail, the capture schedule is paused and the user is notified. The user may resume the schedule manually after resolving the underlying issue. Retries are not guaranteed to succeed if the underlying cause of the failure has not been resolved.

Reporting a Platform Issue

If you believe the capture failure is caused by a bug or issue with the ImmigLens platform (rather than a problem with the source URL), please contact support@immiglens.ca and include your Capture Job ID (#{{ capture_id }}) and a description of the issue. Our engineering team investigates all reported platform-side failures.
