Disclaimer & Important Information

Grace Period Terms

ImmigLens provides a grace period following a payment failure, during which existing account functionality remains available (with the restriction on new position creation noted above). This grace period is provided as a courtesy and does not constitute a waiver of payment obligations. ImmigLens reserves the right to modify the grace period duration for accounts with a history of repeated payment failures.

Account Suspension

If payment is not resolved by the end of the grace period, your account will be suspended. During suspension: all capture schedules are paused, the dashboard becomes read-only, new reports cannot be generated, and access to new features is restricted. Suspended accounts are not deleted immediately — your data is retained for 30 days from the date of suspension, provided a subscription is not reactivated. After 30 days, data may be permanently and irreversibly deleted. ImmigLens will send reminder notifications before data deletion occurs.

Retry Schedule

ImmigLens automatically retries failed payments on a scheduled basis. {{ retries_remaining }} retries remain for this billing cycle. If all retries fail, the subscription is cancelled and the account suspended. To stop the retry cycle and prevent further decline fees from your bank, update your payment method or cancel your subscription from Billing Settings.

Impact on LMIA Evidence

If capture schedules are paused due to account suspension, gaps may appear in your LMIA evidence timeline. ImmigLens is not responsible for evidence gaps resulting from account suspension due to non-payment. If you are in the middle of an active LMIA application period, we strongly recommend resolving the payment issue as quickly as possible to avoid disruption to your evidence capture.

Disputing a Charge

If you believe the payment was declined in error or you have been charged an incorrect amount, contact info@immiglens.ca before initiating a chargeback with your card issuer. Chargebacks may result in immediate account suspension. ImmigLens investigates all billing disputes promptly and will work to resolve legitimate issues within 3 business days.
