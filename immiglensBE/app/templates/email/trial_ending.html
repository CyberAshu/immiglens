Disclaimer & Important Information

Trial Terms

ImmigLens free trials are available to new accounts only. Each account is entitled to one free trial period. The trial provides access to all features of the {% if trial_plan is defined and trial_plan %}{{ trial_plan }}{% else %}trial{% endif %} plan for the stated trial duration. Reports and exports generated during the trial include a visible watermark. ImmigLens reserves the right to modify trial duration and feature access for new accounts at any time.

Post-Trial Data Retention

At the end of the trial period, your account transitions to a read-only state. All positions, evidence, screenshots, and generated reports are retained for 30 days following trial expiry. During this 30-day window, you may activate a subscription to restore full access . all data will be intact. After 30 days, data is permanently and irreversibly deleted. ImmigLens will send a final notification before deletion occurs. ImmigLens is not responsible for data loss resulting from failure to subscribe or reactivate within the 30-day retention window.

All plans are billed monthly or annually (annual plans receive a discount equivalent to two months free). Prices are subject to change . existing subscribers receive 30 days’ advance notice of any price changes. The Founding Member discount, where applicable, is permanent and not subject to price change notices.

Cancellation Policy

Paid subscriptions may be cancelled at any time from Billing Settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. No refunds are issued for unused portions of a billing period. Monthly subscribers can cancel month-to-month with no penalty. Annual subscribers who cancel mid-year are not entitled to a pro-rata refund unless required by applicable consumer protection legislation.
