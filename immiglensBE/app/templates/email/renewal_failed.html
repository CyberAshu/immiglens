Disclaimer & Important Information

Renewal Failure & Grace Period

When a subscription renewal payment fails, ImmigLens enters the account into a grace period. During this period, the account remains functional with the restrictions noted above. The grace period is designed to give users time to resolve payment issues without immediate disruption to their evidence capture workflow.

Automatic Retries

ImmigLens will attempt to process the renewal payment automatically on the retry dates noted above. If a retry succeeds, the subscription is renewed immediately, the grace period ends, and all functionality is fully restored. You will receive a payment confirmation email once the retry succeeds. You do not need to manually trigger a payment after updating your payment method — the next scheduled retry will use the updated method.

Impact on LMIA Applications

If your account is suspended during an active LMIA application period and capture schedules stop, a gap may appear in your evidence timeline. ImmigLens is not responsible for evidence gaps or LMIA application complications resulting from account suspension due to non-payment. If you are in a time-sensitive application period, we strongly recommend resolving the payment issue immediately or manually uploading evidence for any dates that are missed.

Annual Plan Renewal

Annual subscriptions are charged in full at the start of each annual billing period. If a renewal fails, the next billing period does not begin until payment is successfully processed. The renewal date does not shift — if payment succeeds after the original renewal date, the new billing period is backdated to the original renewal date and your subscription continues without a gap.

Cancellation Before Renewal

If you choose to cancel rather than renew, do so from Billing Settings before the grace period ends. Cancellation stops further retry attempts on your payment method and ends your subscription at the end of the current period. Data retention rules apply as described in your subscription terms.
