Phishing & Fraud Warning

ImmigLens will never ask you to share your password, reset link, or any security token via email, phone, or chat. If you receive a message claiming to be from ImmigLens that asks you to confirm your password or provide your reset link, do not respond — it is a phishing attempt. Forward suspicious messages to support@immiglens.ca. All legitimate ImmigLens communications originate from the immiglens.ca domain.

Account Security

If you believe your ImmigLens account has been accessed without your authorization, change your password immediately and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) from Account Settings. You can review all recent login activity and actions in the Audit Log within your account. Contact support@immiglens.ca if you suspect unauthorized access — we can lock the account while you investigate.
