Disclaimer & Important Information

Subscription Terms

By activating this subscription, you agree to ImmigLens’s Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, available at immiglens.ca/legal. Your subscription renews automatically at the end of each billing cycle unless cancelled. Cancellation must be completed before the renewal date to avoid being charged for the next cycle. Subscriptions are subject to ImmigLens’s Fair Use Policy — accounts that significantly exceed normal usage patterns may be subject to review.

Plan Limits & Enforcement

Your plan allows up to {{ position_limit }} active Job Positions at any time. Only positions with a status of Active count toward this limit — archived, paused, and completed positions do not. If you reach the active position limit, you will not be able to create new positions until you either archive an existing one or upgrade your plan. ImmigLens will notify you when you reach 80% of your limit.

Price Changes

ImmigLens may adjust subscription pricing from time to time. Existing subscribers will receive a minimum of 30 days’ written notice before any price change takes effect on their account. You may cancel your subscription before the price change takes effect if you do not wish to continue at the new price. Founding Member discounts are exempt from standard price changes — the discounted rate is locked for Founding Members for the lifetime of the subscription.

Cancellation & Data Retention

You may cancel your ImmigLens subscription at any time from Billing Settings. Upon cancellation, you retain full access until the end of the current billing period. After access ends, your data (positions, evidence, reports) is retained for 30 days. You may reactivate your account during this window and all data will be intact. After 30 days, data is permanently deleted. ImmigLens is not responsible for data loss resulting from account cancellation or expiry.

Hosted in Canada

ImmigLens stores all customer data on infrastructure located in Canada. This includes all screenshot evidence, position data, user account information, and generated reports. ImmigLens does not transfer data to servers outside of Canada. This hosting arrangement is designed to support compliance with PIPEDA and Canadian data residency expectations for immigration-related documentation.
